Why Study in Dubai?

Studying in Dubai comes with a dual benefit of accessing global campuses of universities of world repute and job opportunities of a booming economy at the same time. Institutions in Dubai have affiliations with international universities, offer a wide range of programs suiting your academic pursuits and have world class learning facilities. Dubai is among the fastest growing world economies and has an entrepreneurial ecosystem that creates hundreds of thousands of new jobs in diverse areas. Presence of renowned global universities and a robust economy makes studying in Dubai a very lucrative choice.

Dubai is one of the most popular places for students to study. It has a diverse range of degree options and is an economic hub in the Middle East. There are four main universities in Dubai, including Umm Al-Qura University, Gulf Medical University, Dubai School of Government, and American University of Dubai The United Arab Emirates is a study destination for International Students. It has become an International Education spot as many Universities and colleges are setting up campuses for International Students. Students who wish to study in Dubai require a Study Visa, which is valid for full-time programs.Study in Dubai, a futuristic city that is home to over 60 world renowned university campuses and colleges and that offers plenty of higher education opportunities meeting your aspirations. Pursue a world class undergraduate or postgraduate degree from this destination and advance your career globally.

Dubai is a popular destination for international students due to its world-renowned universities, excellent infrastructure, and diverse opportunities. It is the largest and one of the most populated cities in the United Arab Emirates. Let us understand what makes this city the perfect choice for students who wish to study abroad - The education system in Dubai – The city offers a perfect blend of quality and affordability, which is the dream of every international student. The universities and colleges provide globally competent faculties and a wide variety of learning resources. Some of the top universities of the Gulf region are in Dubai, like Zayed University, University of Dubai, and American University in Dubai (AUD). Middlesex University is globally recognized for producing competent and work-ready graduates. The educational institutions offer challenging academic courses which use a combination of effective traditional and innovative methodology to train the students to meet their goals and requirements. Besides the conventional courses like Business administration and IT, creative courses like property development and management and master’s in Logistics are also offered here.

One can also find branch campuses of reputed universities like BITS Pilani Dubai, University of Wollongong, and others here.

  • Cosmopolitan culture

    • The universities in Dubai have students from more than 100 nationalities, making it easy for international students to integrate. Due to factors like globalization and the internet, there has been a massive influx of people of different nationalities, backgrounds, cultures, religions, and talents in Dubai. Every employer and every universal organization values employees who are tolerant and respectful towards each other despite cultural and demographic differences. The multiculturalism of Dubai helps international students to develop such people skills, which, in turn, makes them more employable.

  • Economy

    • The ultimate aim of most of the international students is to settle down in a place which increases their quality of life and offers them innumerable opportunities to work. Dubai is termed as the city of dreams. It is no secret that Dubai is amongst the fastest growing economies of the world. Also known as the “Entertainment capital of the world,” it provides excellent work opportunities. There are many multinational corporations in Dubai, like the BBC, Microsoft, HP, and so on. Trade and Tourism are the foundation of Dubai’s economy. Needless to say, it is the dream of an international student to get a quality education, a flourishing career, and an incredible standard of life can be easily realized in Dubai.

  • Lifestyle

    • Awe-inspiring architecture and an incredible skyline along with major attractions like the Burj Khalifa and the Dubai Mall, along with many others, make Dubai a dream destination for people – visitors, locals, and international students alike. The beautiful beaches, human-made islands like Palm Jumeira, the Souk Madinat Jumeirah, and so on are always brimming with people. It is safe to say that the city has an endless selection of entertainment of all kinds. International students enjoy not only the perks of a great nightlife of the city but also the therapeutic effects of its flora and fauna. Those who seek adventures can participate in fun activities like diving, desert safaris, and so on.

  • Safety

    • When in Rome, do as the Romans do! This fits perfectly for international students in Dubai. This city is termed as one of the safest cities in the world. The Sharia law prevails in Dubai, which has provided a strict definition of what is allowed and what is not allowed. Adherence to this law has ensured that people behave themselves in public because of which the crime rate is negligent in this city. International students can rest assured that their stay in the city would be pleasant.

Education system in Dubai?

Education structure in Dubai is shown as follows:

  • Primary:

    • At the primary level, English is the medium of instruction in most of the schools in Dubai. There are also large numbers of schools which teach children in other foreign languages like, French, Hindi, even Russian. There is also a wide range of foreign curriculum's, from British National Curriculum to Japanese system. For admission, some schools conduct entrance tests and most of the good schools also have a long waiting list. The new term in most of British schools in Dubai starts in September. In all other schools’ new term begins in April. During July and August all schools are closed for holidays. School uniforms are usually similar to European and American Schools' uniform. In most of the school’s timings are from 8 am to 2 pm with usually two or three breaks. Average Fees is approximately Dhs. 3000 in schools. Primary section is usually of six years and age level is from 6 to 12 years.

  • Secondary:

    • Dubai also has large number of international high schools which offers to expatriates as well as domicile residents large scale of curriculum's- from Indian CBSE to IGCSE. School uniform is similar to those in primary. Compulsory subjects in most of the school include Maths, Social Studies, Physical Education, Religion and Arabic. Pupil can choose subjects such as Science, Languages, Sociology, History, Computer Studies etc. The offered subjects depend on school education system. An average fee in private schools is varying up to Dhs 25000. Secondary school period is generally of three years and age level from 12 to 18 years. Certificate or diploma awarded by Secondary School Leaving Certificate.

  • Colleges and Universities in Dubai:

    • The number of colleges and universities in Dubai has grown significantly in last 20 years and every year the number of their staff and students increases by about 30%. Most of the colleges and universities are affiliating themselves to prestigious institutions abroad, and many of them use English as tuition language.

  • Postgraduate Education in Dubai:

    • A large number of Dubai's universities also offer various Master and PhD postgraduate programs. To learn more about postgraduate programs in Dubai and see a full list of universities and institutes that offer master and PhD programs to study in Dubai for Indian students. Technical Secondary School Length of programme is about 6 years and age level is from 12 to 18 years. Certificate/diploma awarded: Technical Secondary Diploma. Dubai has an extensive education sector. Government in Dubai offers free education from kindergarten to university to all its citizens and about 40% of children attend private schools. For all children older than 5 years full-time education is compulsory and all primary and secondary schools in Dubai, public and private, up to ninth grade also have to follow universal and compulsory program bases.

  • Courses Offered At Undergraduate levels:

    • Business, Management, Accounting, Finance, Human Resources Management, Hospitality Management, Tourism, Psychology with counselling, Global social science, Advertising, Public Relations, Media, Business Information Systems, Information Technology, Early Childhood Education, Fashion Designing, Civil Engineering. Duration for the undergraduate program will be 3 to 4 years.

  • At Postgraduate levels:

    • Overseas education in Dubai, is also popular through the medium of the below given courses: MBA, Retailing, Advertising, Supply Chain Management, International Business, Petroleum Engineering, International Relations, Quality Management, Engineering Management, Strategic Marketing, Human Resources Management, Marketing Communications., Marketing.

Careers & Industry Insights

Second wealthiest emirate in the UAE, Dubai is known as the top business doorway for African and middle eastern countries. Top sector that drives growth of this economy is tourism though Dubai is fast developing an epicentre for service industries including Information Technology and Finance. Dubai Internet City and Dubai Media City are known for housing top IT firms and media organizations such as Microsoft, Oracle Corporation, IBM, HP, BBC, CNN and Sky News. Top jobs/sectors to look forward in Dubai include E-Commerce, Digital Marketing Specialists, Business Development & Sales, Education and Engineering & Technology.

Study in Dubai for Indian students enables them to work part time for 20 hours a week in free zone areas. They are required to get the permission from universities. Students are allowed to work in an internship program after getting permission from their university. Some universities have placement offices in Dubai that help students find internships. The salary received while working can be anywhere between 15-30 AED per hour, i.e. 273 - 546 INR per hour. Salaries can range from anything between AED 36000 to AED 810,000, i.e. 656,000 - 14,760,500 INR, depending on the field of study. The minimum employment age in Dubai/UAE is 18 years. If you get an internship offer then you are allowed to work as an intern in Dubai else you are not allowed to do so on a student visa. Approach the UAE Labor department to obtain a permit which will allow you to take up part-time work in Dubai, legally.

Top courses to study inDubai?

Study in Dubai for Indian students, usually ranges to the below given popular courses:

  • MBA

    • The Master of Business Administration (MBA) is an internationally-recognized degree, designed to develop the skills required for careers in business and management. It can also be useful in the public sector, government, private industry, and other areas. To study MBA in Dubai includes Accounting, Economics, Marketing, and Operations, as well as elective courses that allow participants to follow their own personal or professional interests.

  • Engineering

    • Engineering is favourite to study in Dubai for Indian students because of the challenging career opportunities and professionalism linked with it. A career in Engineering gives you the flexibility and a wide variety of choices for expansion.

  • Accounts and Finance

    • Accounting is the systematic recording of financial transactions related to a business. It refers to the process of summarizing, analysing and reporting these transactions to oversight agencies and tax collection entities. Finance involves the creation, management and study of money, banking, credit, investments, assets and liabilities.

  • Tourism and Hospitality

    • Tourism and hospitality cover the fundamentals of each industry including Tourism Economics, Food Service, and Legal Issues. This course can help you develop the management and entrepreneurship skills necessary to work in establishments related to the field.

  • Architecture

    • The profession of Building Design, open areas, communities, and other artificial constructions and environments. Architecture often includes design or selection of furnishings and decorations, supervision of construction work, and the examination, restoration, or remodelling of existing buildings. Built environment refers to the man-made surroundings that provide the setting for human activity, ranging in scale from buildings and parks or green space to neighbourhood and cities that can often include their supporting infrastructure, such as water supply or energy networks.

  • Aviation

    • Aviation is the practical aspect or art of aeronautics, being the design, development, production, operation and use of aircraft, especially heavier-than-air aircraft.

  • Design Studies

    • Design is a work process which has a user prospect and stimulates development based on your specific customers’ needs. A plan or drawing used to show the look, function or workings of a building, garment, or other object before it is made.
