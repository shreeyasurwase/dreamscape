Why Study in Singapore?

One of the most popular destinations students consistently chose for their studies abroad is Singapore. While this may sound surprising at first, once you learn about everything Singapore has to offer and the many opportunities for students, it seems like the obvious choice! First, Singapore was ranked as the 6th best city in Asia by QS Best Student Cities in 2022. It is also a leading global destination for business, innovation, and education. In fact, Singapore is well-known for its high-quality universities and exceptional student life conditions.

Singapore is also included, along with Switzerland, in the top two countries in the world for quality of life for expats, which means students will benefit from studying abroad in these locations.

Work Permit for Singapore

International students once securing their degree in MS Singapore are required to apply for a work visa. There are two types of categories under work visas that students must check before applying.

Quick facts

  • Skilled and Semi-Skilled: The first category of visa is called S pass which is required for individuals to work in Singapore coming from various nationalities. This particular visa is for a category that comes under skilled or semi-skilled and to obtain this category of visa, individuals must demonstrate a minimum of 2,200 SGD income.
  • Professional Workers: Some individuals who are willing to run their own business in Singapore rather than jobs are required to apply for Employment Pass. An earning of 3,300 SGD and an executive position can apply for this kind of work permit.

    • Individuals can easily apply for any of the following visas, when they are looking for future earnings in Singapore.

    Popular Online Job Portals

    • Beam is an online platform that helps employers and employees contact each other. Candidates can look for job postings and employers can post jobs according to various filters like post, location and salary package.
    • Jobs DB- It is one of the most common online job search portals. Thousands of jobs are posted on the platform and candidates can apply according to their filters.
    • Job street- It is another popular platform similar to Jobs DB. Thousands of employers ranging from small enterprises to MNC’s post roles and candidates can filter their search according to their preferred location, salary and job type.
    • MyCareersFuture.sg- A government owned website connecting thousands of employers and employees together.

      • Level of education

      Primary Education

      School Education is a priority in well regulated Singapore and follows the core western model. The first 4 years of primary education form a foundation stage with a common curriculum. During the 2 year orientation phase that follows though, pupils are streamed per subject according to their learning abilities, or difficulties.

      Secondary Education

      Students are streamed at secondary schools too, but this time in terms of their primary school leaving examinations. The special and express tracks are 4-year programs leading to Cambridge GCE O Levels. The normal stream may take a student on a 5 year journey to the same destination but via N Levels, with choices of following academic or technical curricular. In all cases, the values of the outcomes are the same. The main difference is the time taken to qualify.

      Vocational Education

      Vocational training is provided by the institute of technical training. The government has pledged the equivalent of 1.6 billion US dollars to staff it up with more competent teachers, so that 1 in 4 of its students enter a polytechnic, instead of the current 1 in 5.

      Tertiary Education

      Students who completed their GCE O Levels may proceed directly to polytechnics. To enter university though, they must first pass their A Levels after studying for 2 years more at a pre-university. There are 5 polytechnics in Singapore, and 3 autonomous universities responsible for their own curricula. Of the latter, the National University of Singapore illustrated here is the oldest, having been established in 1960 on the foundations of the Singapore Division of Kuala Lumpur University.
